# Tennis Goals — Pro Player Journey

## Ultimate Goal
Become a professional tennis player competitive at ITF/ATP Challenger level.

## Current Level
Amateur / beginner (establishing baseline — started 2026-04-27)

## Milestone Targets
- [ ] Consistent forehand and backhand (>75% success rate in practice)
- [ ] Reliable serve (>70% first serve in)
- [ ] Net play proficiency (>65% volley success rate)
- [ ] Win local club tournaments
- [ ] Achieve NTRP rating 4.5+
- [ ] Enter ITF Futures events
- [ ] Rank in national amateur circuit
- [ ] ATP Challenger-level competition

## Training Philosophy
- Physical fitness is the foundation — recovery and injury prevention first
- Technical consistency before power
- Mental resilience built through match-play experience
- Nutrition and sleep are non-negotiable performance levers
- Track every session; data drives improvement

## Key Physical Benchmarks for Pro Level
- Resting HR: < 50 bpm
- HRV: > 60 ms consistently
- Sleep: 8-9 hours minimum
- Steps: 10,000+ on training days
